About Us

Our Sitter List Nashville was formed in 2015 by two working moms of four active children under the age of seven. Whitney Schickling and Karly Hart (the founders) also happened to be sisters-in-law and best friends. For them, finding trusting baby sitters was a major headache in juggling child care with their busy work and social schedules. Many of their Nashville friends shared the same frustration. Fueled by this need along with their entrepreneurial spirits and trust for each other, they launched Our Sitter List LLC, the parent of www.oursitterlistnashville.com.

Important Things About Our Sitter List

  • The founders screen all potential sitter members .
  • All sitter members and parent members are subject to a background check.
  • All sitter members and parent members provide references that the founders verify
  • All sitter members must be CPR certified within 60 days of signing up with www.oursitterlistnashville.com
